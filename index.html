How stresscow became disabeled

For many months stresscow has lived on our table.
Every now and then someone would pay some attention to her. This attention, however, cost her her legs.
With this severe mutilation, she could no longer fit in with the layout of the table.
Now stresscow is condemned to spend her days alone, wandering through the most forgotten corners of the house.
